«FRUMINT HOOKAH» tobacco-free fruit and mint molasses is a unique innovation in hookah smoking culture that provides the healthiest tobacco alternative.

Typically, when we hear hookah, nargileh, argileh, or qalyan, the first thought that comes to mind is shisha tobacco.

Hookah tobacco is made from fermenting tobacco leaves with honey, molasses, or glycerine together with flavoring to create a product that's sweeter and smoother than just tobacco on its own.

Tobacco leaves contain the highly addictive stimulant nicotine, which more and more shisha smokers are trying to avoid. Until recently, there were very few alternatives to shisha tobacco; however, various herbal and fruit options are entering the hookah market.

"FRUMINT HOOKAH" is the best option for those who want to get away from tobacco and nicotine.

We have replaced harmful tobacco with fruits, mint, and honey.

Our production technology creates the perfect tobacco substitute by transforming natural healthy ingredients into an excellent fruit-mint blend.

A completely different, more natural, and exclusive concept - premium quality fresh and deep flavor.
