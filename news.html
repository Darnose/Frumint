Assembling a hookah: what does it consist of?

Many hookah lovers want to buy a hookah home to be able to enjoy aromatic smoke at any time. It is not only more convenient, you don't have to go anywhere, but also more profitable. Even if you buy an expensive hookah from famous brands, it will quickly pay for itself.

Before choosing a hookah, you need to find out what it consists of. We will talk about the main parts of the hookah.

Construction of hookah

The main components are a shaft , a flask and a bowl. But no less important are accessories - kalaud, ports for hoses and others. We will tell you in detail about each component and help you choose it.

  • The shaft

    The shaft is the most important part of the hookah. Smoke passes and cools through it, and all harmful substances are retained in it. The shaft is not only the most important part, but also the most expensive, and you should not save on it. There are prefabricated, cast and component shafts.

    Optimal height: 60-70 cm

    Material: metal, light stainless steel

  • The flask

    The flask should be heavy, stable. The wider the bottom of the flask, the more convenient it is to use.

    Material: strong glass, metal or plastic

  • A bowl

    A bowl is a place where tobacco is placed. It also plays an important role in the final result. You can buy domestic bowls or Egyptian-made bowls. But the best option is a bowl made of clay with a heat-resistant enamel coating. Such models are a little more expensive, but they are much better than cheap silicone counterparts.

    Material: ceramic clay, silicone, glass, metal

  • A hose

    Hoses that connect to the mine. Everything is simpler here, it is better to take an ordinary silicone hose of the length you need. There are beautiful and stylish options for hoses based on a metal winding, but they are not so convenient. Hoses with metal can be covered in textiles or leather, but due to constant contact with water, the metal will oxidize, which is harmful.

    Material: silicone

  • A mouthpiece

    A mouthpiece is a pipe that a smoker holds in his hands. There is a wide choice here. But glass ones are quite fragile, wooden ones absorb the smell. The best option is tubes made of good plastic or metal.

    Material: plastic, wood, glass, metal

  • A saucer for the bowl

  • Tongs

  • Kalaud (analog of foil)

You can buy a ready-made hookah, it is faster and easier, or you can assemble it from the components. It will take longer, but you will get exactly what you wanted, and the end result is usually cheaper.

The assembly is simple: first, the flask is filled with liquid (water, milk, alcohol or juice), the flask and shaft are connected (the shaft should sink only 2-3 cm), then a saucer and a bowl with tobacco are installed. Connect the hose to the port. The hookah is ready, the main thing is to check the tightness of the connections. The assembly is intuitive.
